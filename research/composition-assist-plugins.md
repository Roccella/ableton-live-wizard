# Research - Composition Assist Tools (March 2026)

Date: 2026-03-06

## What was researched
- Composition assistant products/plugins adjacent to proposed workflow.
- How existing tools balance guided composition vs user control.
- Historical AI tooling inside Ableton ecosystem (Magenta Studio).

## Key Findings
- Scaler 3 strongly validates demand for guided harmonic workflow with practical arrangement utilities.
- Existing composition tools often excel at harmony ideation, but less at deep DAW-native real-time control loops.
- Ableton's Magenta Studio post is old (2019) but still relevant as proof that:
- MIDI-focused AI assistance inside Live is practical.
- "continue/interpolate/variation" style transformations are useful primitives.
- Opportunity remains for an agent that combines:
- conversational intent capture,
- live-safe DAW mutation,
- guided staged workflow (note->chord->progression->arrangement).

## Competitive Implications
- Do not compete on "infinite generation" in v1.
- Compete on workflow speed, reversibility, and structured progression in-session.
- Keep first genre/key flows explicit and deterministic where possible.
- Include explicit variation primitives early (continue/interpolate/transform ideas) rather than only raw generation.
