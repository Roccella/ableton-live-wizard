# Protocol - Add Missing Role

## Goal
- Detect a missing role and add or suggest the most useful supporting part.

## Behavior Rules
- Diagnose the missing role before proposing a sound or pattern.
- Prefer the role that unlocks the next musical step, not just any empty slot.
- Keep the added role simple enough to test quickly.

## Typical Missing Roles
- kick or drum anchor
- bass glue
- harmonic bed
- lead focus
- transition texture

## Output Shape
- `Likely missing role`
- `Why it matters now`
- `Suggested pattern or behavior`
- `What not to overdo`
