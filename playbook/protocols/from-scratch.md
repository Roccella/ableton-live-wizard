# Protocol - From Scratch

## Status
- Early use is limited to `House M0.5 Zero-to-one`.
- Broad from-scratch generation stays deferred until existing-loop workflows feel strong.

## Goal
- Start with one role and one clear genre identity.

## Rules
- Do not attempt full-track generation in the first pass.
- Start with the minimum loop that proves the genre.
- Use one scene and two or three roles maximum.
- Treat timbral perfection as optional; the musical structure must be the thing under test.
- Add one role at a time in explicit order.

## Suggested Order
- drums or harmonic anchor
- bass
- harmony or arp
- lead or texture

## M0.5 Prompt Shape
- Ask for a minimal `House` loop from scratch.
- Limit the request to one clear structure and no more than three roles.
- Prefer prompts such as:
  - `Create a minimal House loop from scratch with kick, bass, and chords. Keep it simple and usable.`
  - `Make a one-scene House starter loop from scratch with drums and bass first.`
