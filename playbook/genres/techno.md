# Genre - Techno

## Identity
- Repetition, hypnosis, pressure, texture, and controlled evolution.

## Typical Traits
- 4x4 pulse remains central.
- Groove often comes from subtle percussion and modulation rather than obvious chord motion.
- Harmonic content can be sparse but timbral motion matters a lot.

## Arrangement Clues
- Sections may be defined by density, texture, and filter movement more than by chord change.
- Good suggestions often preserve the pattern while altering tension.

## Common Mistakes
- Treating techno like house with fewer notes.
- Overexplaining harmony when texture is the real lever.
- Adding too much melodic spotlight too early.
