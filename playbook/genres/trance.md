# Genre - Trance

## Identity
- Melodic uplift, atmosphere, long-form tension and release, and emotionally legible harmony.

## Typical Traits
- Arps, pads, leads, and risers have clearer structural roles than in house or techno.
- Harmonic movement and section architecture matter more.
- Builds and releases should feel earned and directional.

## Arrangement Clues
- Breaks and lifts often need stronger contrast than in house.
- Texture, harmony, and anticipation usually matter more than groove alone.

## Common Mistakes
- Making it feel like generic EDM without a melodic center.
- Too much density too early.
- Hard-driving energy with no emotional release.
