# Genre - Drum And Bass

## Identity
- Fast tempo, syncopated drum language, bass design importance, and sharper contrast.

## Typical Traits
- Groove is not 4x4-first.
- Drums carry more genre identity than in most house/trance contexts.
- Bass movement and texture often define section energy.

## Arrangement Clues
- Energy shifts can be more abrupt.
- Drum function should be evaluated before suggesting harmonic expansion.

## Common Mistakes
- Applying house logic to rhythm and section pacing.
- Overcrowding the low end.
- Ignoring drum phrasing while focusing only on notes and harmony.
