# Genre - House

## Identity
- 4x4 pulse, groove-first low end, clear pocket, concise harmonic content.

## Typical Traits
- BPM usually in the mid-120s.
- Kick is stable and foundational.
- Bass and kick must feel like one groove system.
- Chords, stabs, or pads often define mood more than busy melody.

## Arrangement Clues
- Small changes can create useful sections.
- Breaks often thin drums and widen harmony.
- Lifts often come from tension and anticipation more than from maximal note density.

## Common Mistakes
- Too many melodic ideas at once.
- Bass that competes with the kick.
- Overbuilt breaks that lose groove identity.
