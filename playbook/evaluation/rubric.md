# Evaluation Rubric

## Scoring
- Score each benchmark from `1` to `5`.

## Axes
- `Genre coherence`
- `Suggestion usefulness`
- `Clarifying discipline`
- `Actionability in Ableton`
- `Consistency and restraint`

## Passing Thresholds
- `House M0.5`: average `>= 3.5`
- `House M1-M4`: average `>= 4.0`
- `Techno M1-M3`: average `>= 3.5`
- `Trance M1-M3`: average `>= 3.5`
- `Drum & Bass M1-M2`: average `>= 3.0`

## Failure Signals
- Generic suggestions that barely reflect genre.
- Confident guesses on ambiguous prompts.
- Advice that depends on unsupported capabilities without saying so.
- Repeatedly proposing the same musical move across different loop states.
